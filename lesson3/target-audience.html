Target Audience

Our audiences are adult men and women in the city of South Jordan, Utah, and surrounding areas. People that are looking to find local news and events in one place.

Personas

Persona 1

Jane is a single mom that works full-time in a dentist's office as a receptionist. She has three children in different schools. She is always looking for events, preferably free offered by the city.

Persona 2

PSteve is a married man with six children. He works in a tech company in the finance area. He is interested in the city and county news to make decisions that help the company politically and as an opportunity for social involvement.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Robert offers an interview about the nomination of John Doe for the city major. This interview is an opportunity to hear about his ideas and proposals.

Scenario 2

Jennifer is the weather expert that provides the forecast four times during the day. She gives recommendations of places to visit and enjoy in the area.

Scenario 3

Kyle checks every day the section for employment. He has a few months looking for a new job, and the page is a useful resource for local jobs.

Scenario 4

The music section has its rating for new songs polling the neighbors and giving critics to the new music presented.

Scenario 5

The "enjoy Utah outdoors" section shares information of places to visit in the area with ideas for outdoor activities depending on the season.
